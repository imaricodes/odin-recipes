Tuna Salad

Description

A suite of simple ingredients make this tuna salad an easy choice when time is short and hunger is high. Chicken is a substitute that works well if tuna is not your thing.

Tip: It may not sound good on paper, but finely chopped apples or dried cranberries in place of the sweet relish may pleasantly surprise you!

Ingredients

  • Albacore Tuna (4 12-oz cans)
  • White Onion (281 g)
  • Celery (186 g)
  • Red Onion (90 g)
  • Sweet Relish (add to taste)
  • Mayonnaise (add to desired consistency)

Steps

  1. Crumble tuna in a large bowl.
  2. Add diced onion and celery.
  3. Blend mixture.
  4. Add mayonnaise. The amount is personal. Add and stir until you're satisfied.
  5. Add sweet relish. Follow the same instructions for mayonnaise.
