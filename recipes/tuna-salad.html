Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • Albacore Tuna (4 cans)
  • White Onion (281 g)
  • Celery (186 g)
  • Red Onion (90 g)
  • Sweet Relish (add to taste)
  • Mayonnaise (add to desired consistency)

Steps

  1. Crumble Tuna in a large bowl.
  2. Add diced onion and celery
  3. Blend mixture
  4. Add mayonnaise. The amount is personal. Add and stir until you're satisfied.
  5. Add sweet relish. Follow the same instructions for mayonnaise.
